Accumulation of Learning English [05]

How to be diplomatic (soften your language).

In some situations, you should be diplomatic so that you don’t offend anyone.

  1. Soften the language.
  2. Using modal verbs.
  3. Using negative questions.

1、This isn’t good.

  • I think this could be improved.
  • Why don’t we make some changes to this?
  • Wouldn’t this be better if …?

2、I don’t like it.

  • I’m not a big fan of …
  • I’m not too keen on …
  • …isn’t really to my taste.
  • …isn’t my cup of tea.
  • …isn’t my favourite.

3、You’re wrong.

  • I’m not sure that’s entirely accurate.

4、I’m not satisfied with …

  • This isn’t exactly what I had in my mind.
  • I have some reservations.

5、Would you like to …

  • I’m afraid I can’t at the moment. Actually, I need to …

6、Can I speak to you?

  • I’m a bit busy right now, could you give me a minute?
  • That’s not a bad idea, but …. How about …?

Do/Did you Know …?

What’s the difference between “Did you know” and “Do you know”?

Do you know means we don’t know something(the answer) but we want to get some infoamation.

Examples:

  1. Do you know where I can buy nice shoes?
  2. Do you know what time it is?

Did you know means I know this and I want to tell you a fact. I want to see you know the fact.

Examples:

  1. Did you know Mt.everst is the world’s tallest mountain?
  2. Did you know the most common letter in English is “e”?

Phrasal Verbs: Add “OUT” to change the meaning of these 8 verbs

Course Links: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Cr4RYby4L6Q

  • Looking out is used to mean be careful(Look out! The car is coming!). On the other hand, Look out means paying attention to do something(I will look out for you!).
  • Staying out means to remain out for a long time(He stayed out all night).
  • Pointing out means to draw your attention to something(My teacher always points out my mistakes carefully).
  • Working out means exercising or solving questions.
  • Wearing out means something can’t be used anymore it’s no longer usable. On the other hand, it means you’re so tired(My T-shirt is wearing out. / Today I’m so tired, I am wearing out.).
  • Ruling out means you eliminate the possibility(You can rule out possibilities or answers).
  • Standing out means something or some people are noticeable
  • Finding out means discovering or learning(I need to find out when the next bus leaves).

Preposition tips

Psychology of investing

  1. rational, 理性的. emotional: 情绪化的. fear, adj.害怕的. greed, adj.贪婪的.

  2. dip one’s toes into something: 正尝试做某事. I heard my favorite friend is dipping his toes into investing.

  3. I suggest …. as well: 我建议……. I suggest you can learn some new skills about your work as well.

  4. In my humble opinion, 在我看来. In my humble opinion, they’re ultimately driven by emotions like fear and greed.

  5. a pretty significant edge, 一个很大的优势. Understanding this will give you a pretty significant edge.

  6. playing the long game, 做长期打算. Emotional states can make us forget we’re playing the long game.

  7. Looks like I’ve got some homework to do, 看来我还有一些功课需要去做.

Children with Autism

Words

  1. autism: n.自闭症
  2. coping mechanisms: 应对机制. A lot of people use alcohol as a coping mechanism against hard times.
  3. overload: n. 超载, 过重的负担. Nobody says that money is overload. / When I get into this meeting, people dump lots of information on me. I can’t process it, so it’s information overload for me.
  4. stimulation: n. 刺激; 鼓励; 鼓舞. stimming: n. 自我刺激行为.

Phrase

  1. information gap: There is a huge information gap out there. And when we’re dealing with little kids at school, sometimes an information gap can be really dangerous thing.
  2. Adjusting to a new environment: 适应新环境.
  3. somebody’s first encounter with…: 第一次遇到…….
  4. That’s great advice!

中秋节重装网站

其实应该有一段时间了，我发现我的网站根目录下总是会多出来一些PHP文件，然后这些文件内会对我的网站进行重定向到一些垃圾网站。

[photos]

[/photos]

除此之外，还发现了网站的一个小BUG，在移动端访问本网站的时候Pjax刷新会卡死无限循环，不会跳转也不会自动刷新页面。因此我决定趁着中秋节的时间，给网站来个大的“体检”。

经过了几天的摸查，将网站的安全防护机制又增高了一点，所有后台密码也进行了更换。同时针对那个BUG，也发现是在根目录下的config.inc.php中出现的，由于之前自己增加了一句可以解析QQ头像作为评论头像的代码而触发了这个BUG，而且这个BUG在PC端不会出现，只在移动端会出现，而自己平时又基本都是使用PC访问博客，因此就导致很长时间了才发现这个BUG，真心对不住这段时间使用移动端访问我博客的那些网友！

关于这次网站整顿做了一次总结：中秋节网站重新上线，毕竟下次再进行整顿不知道又是猴儿年马月了，还是自己先做个笔记，免得之后又要一个个查。

除了修补BUG之外，本次中秋节期间对网站整体静态资源迁移到了七牛云CDN，网站整体访问速度得到很大的提升。

「运动手表轨迹」Google Earth制作骑行路线

本文介绍下如何制作户外电子设备常见运动轨迹文件，有些也会称之为路书，其实本质就是一个GPS或者KML文件。

首先打开谷歌地球网站：https://earth.google.com

创建项目文件

然后点击左侧项目：

接着点击新项目：

在新项目里创建KML文件（KML文件好像是可以与GPS文件相互转换的？）：

规划路线

规划路线很简单，只需要动动鼠标即可。点击创建线条或形状：

接着按照提示画出路线，双击鼠标左键或者回车键结束。

导出文件

点击左上角三个点图标，即可将项目文件导出为KML文件。

至此，我们已经得到了轨迹文件，接下来就是如何在运动手表端打开导入这个文件。

导入路线到华米运动手表

  1. 电脑微信分享文件到手机；
  2. 在手机端选择可以识别GPS/KML文件的软件（例如：悦跑圈、Zepp）；
  3. 打开后保存至软件，然后导入至设备。
